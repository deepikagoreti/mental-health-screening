CBT Tools

Academic & Exam Stress Tools

Strategies to overcome studying blocks, exam panic, and time-management burnout:

  • Pomodoro Technique: Work for 25 minutes, break for 5. Helps focus when anxious.
  • Cognitive Restructuring: Replace "I will fail" with "I am preparing the best I can, and one test doesn't seal my future."
  • Study Breaks: A 10-minute walk in nature relaxes the amygdala.
