The 4-7-8 Breathing Technique

Also known as the "relaxing breath," this exercise acts as a natural tranquilizer for the nervous system.

  • Inhale (4 Seconds)

    Breathe in quietly through your nose, letting your belly expand.

  • Hold (7 Seconds)

    Keep your breath suspended comfortably without straining.

  • Exhale (8 Seconds)

    Exhale completely through your mouth, making a gentle 'whoosh' sound.
